During one of my 5th semester classes, we worked with a charity organization called BAMX that helps people in need by providing them with food and other essential needs. We were tasked with making an app that would help them in some way. My team and i decided to make an app that would help the organization better keep track of food deliveries and donations, while also giving volunteer workers a companion app that would help them track their deliveries and report them.

Here you can find a demo of the app we made for the organization, it was made by using Swift and Xcode, we used Firebase to host our real-time database. I worked mainly in the back end of the site, creating the main functionality of delivery assignment, report creation, admin and volunteer user differences and pretty much everything related with the logic of the app. My team mainly worked with the authentication system and with the design of the application.
